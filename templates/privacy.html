1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Task Mama ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to ensuring the confidentiality and security of your personal data and maintaining transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Task Data: Details about your tasks, goals, and reminders
  • User Content: Categories, tags, notes, and other data you add to tasks
  • Payment Information: Processed by Apple via the App Store (we do not store payment details)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Technical Data: App performance, crash reports, and error logs
  • Location Data: General location (if granted permission) for task reminders and suggestions

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Apple Services: Account verification and purchase information from Apple
  • Task Management Services: Third-party services for task reminders and synchronization

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Service Delivery: To process and store your tasks and provide reminder functionality
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account and authenticate your access
  • Premium Features: To provide subscription-based services and features
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance

3.2 Additional Uses

  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns to enhance app features and performance
  • Communication: To send service-related notifications, updates, and marketing communications (if opted in)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Task data is securely stored on encrypted servers
  • Data Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as required for legal purposes
  • Backup Systems: Your data is regularly backed up to prevent loss

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls are enforced to limit who can access your data
  • Security Monitoring: We regularly monitor our systems to detect potential vulnerabilities
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use industry-standard security practices and technologies

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

5.2 We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help us operate the App (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets
  • Safety: To protect the safety and rights of users, our company, or others

5.3 Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and subscription management
  • Analytics Services: We may share anonymized usage data for app improvement
  • Cloud Providers: We may share data with trusted cloud providers for secure hosting

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Account Access: You can view and edit your account information within the App
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of your data
  • Data Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all data associated with it
  • Subscription Management: You can manage subscriptions via your Apple ID settings

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control notifications within the App and via device settings
  • Marketing: Opt-out of promotional communications while still receiving service-related notifications

6.3 Location Data

  • Permission Control: Grant or revoke location access through device settings
  • Usage: Location data is used only for task-related features and reminders if permission is granted

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries outside your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

9. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain data while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Data is deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain some data longer for legal compliance
  • Anonymized Data: Some data may be anonymized and retained for analytical purposes

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through an in-app notification, email notice, or other means. The date of the last update will be reflected at the top of this policy.

11. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

  • Right to Know: You can request information about the personal data we collect and its use
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of the sale of your personal data (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on consent, contract, or legitimate interests
  • Data Portability: You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data
  • Supervisory Authority: You can contact your local data protection authority with concerns
